Covid Guidelines

  • To ensure safety, the seating capacity is reduced to ⅓ for the complete building
  • As per new operating norms, there is no allocated workplace / office and booking  needs to be renewed manually every day  
  • Social distancing norms of having 2m distance between occupied seats is required (E.g.  adjacent seats cannot be booked)
  • A seat can be occupied only if it is sanitized. After an occupant has left a seat,  housekeeping will sanitize seat / office after office hours and update seat status  digitally
  • Conference rooms will be sanitized within 1 hour after usage 
  • Common areas (Pantry, Recreational Areas, Lobbies etc.) will be sanitized after every  4 hours
